Tonnage = Tonnage * Squad Size
Squad Size = 5 for Clan, 4 for IS, 6 for WoB and Comstar.

Base Melee Damage is:
    2-5 Tons = 5 * Squad Size
    6-9 Tons = 10 * Squad Size
    10-15 Tons = 15 * Squad Size

Armor and Structure Values are:
    Armor = TT max * 5
    Structure = TT max * 5

Slots per Proto:
    5 + Hard mounted slots
